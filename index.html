About

Welcome to my homepage! My name is Xun Huang (黄勋 in Chinese, pronounced as /shuun hwang/). I am a research scientist at NVIDIA. I obtained my PhD from the Department of Computer Science at Cornell University, affiliated with Cornell Tech at NYC. I was advised by Professor Serge Belongie. My research focuses on deep generative models and applying them to re-create the visual world.

During PhD, my research was supported by Adobe Research Fellowship (2019), Snap Research Fellowship (2019), and NVIDIA Graduate Fellowship (2018).
