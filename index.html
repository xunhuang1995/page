About

Welcome to my homepage! My name is Xun Huang (黄勋 in Chinese, pronounced as /shuun hwang/). I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Cornell University, affiliated with Cornell Tech at NYC. I am advised by Professor Serge Belongie. My research is focused on deep generative models and applying them to re-create the visual world.

My research has been supported by Adobe Research Fellowship (2019), Snap Research Fellowship (2019), and NVIDIA Graduate Fellowship (2018).
